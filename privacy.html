AIMee Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

AIMee ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, process, and share your information when you use the AIMee mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address or phone number
  • User ID

1.2 Profile Information

  • Nickname
  • First name / Last name (if provided)
  • Gender (if provided)
  • Age or year of birth (if provided)
  • Location information (if provided)

1.3 Chat & AI Interaction Data

  • Text messages between you and AI companions
  • Voice messages (if you use voice features)
  • AI-generated responses

2. AI Processing & Third-Party LLM Providers

To provide AI chat and companion functionality, AIMee transmits certain user data to third-party Large Language Model (LLM) providers for processing.

These providers may include:

  • OpenAI
  • Google Gemini
  • DeepSeek

The following data may be transmitted for AI response generation:

  • Chat messages you send
  • Nickname
  • First and last name (if provided)
  • Gender (if provided)
  • Age or year of birth (if provided)
  • Location (if provided)

This data is used solely for:

  • Generating AI responses
  • Maintaining conversational continuity
  • Safety and abuse prevention

We require these providers to use data only to provide AI processing services to AIMee and to maintain appropriate security safeguards.

3. User Consent

AIMee will request your explicit consent before transmitting personal data to AI service providers. You may withdraw your consent at any time in the App settings. If consent is withdrawn, AI chat functionality may become unavailable.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We share data only with:

  • AI processing providers listed above
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Security and compliance services

5. Data Retention

  • Chat data is retained only as long as necessary to provide services
  • You may request deletion of your account and data
  • Anonymized data may be retained for system improvement

6. Data Security

We use encryption (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and monitoring to protect your data.

7. International Data Transfers

AI providers may process data in different countries. By using AIMee, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred internationally in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request deletion
